MS Dhoni

About MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni is a former Indian international cricketer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen in the history of cricket. Dhoni captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2016 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.

Achievements

  • Led India to victory in the 2007 ICC World Twenty20
  • Guided India to win the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups
  • Captained India to win the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup
  • Helped India win the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy

Records

  • Holds the record for most wins as captain in T20Is
  • First wicket-keeper to effect 150 stumpings in ODIs
  • Highest individual score by a wicket-keeper in ODIs
  • Holds the record for most dismissals by an Indian wicket-keeper
